Delicious Boiled Water

Delicious boiled water recipe that can be used for anything such as pasta or making a nice broth.

Ingredients

  • A medium or large pot
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps and Preparation

  1. Grab a medium or large pot and fill about halfway with water.
  2. Put the pot of water on the stove and turn the burner onto medium-high heat.
  3. Heat the water on the stove and add your salt and pepper.
  4. Watch the water and once you see big bubbles start to come up throughout the pot you know you have boiling water.
  5. Finish the recipe by using the boiling water for whatever you like.
