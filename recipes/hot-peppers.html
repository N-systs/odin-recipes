Bacon Wrapped Chili Peppers Recipe

The most delicious cooked chilis or jalapenos you could ever have. Perfect addition to any dinner and it is a wonderful recipe for any week night or a weekend barbeque.

Ingredients

  • 10-12 Medium size chili or Jalapeno peppers
  • 2 packs of cream cheese
  • A bag of shredded cheddar or any other shredded cheese you prefer
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • One pack of thin sliced bacon
  • A pack of tooth picks (if you are going to grill the peppers)

Steps and Instructions

  1. Take all the peppers and make sure to rinse them of any dirt or contaminants.
  2. Grab a medium sized kitchen knife and cut the top and bottoms of the peppers halfway through.
  3. Now, make a lengthwise cut meeting the slice made on the top and bottom of the peppers.
  4. Once all the peppers have been sliced, use your thumb to pry open the cuts and remove all the seeds and internal parts of the peppers.
  5. After all the seeds and insides are removed, grab a mixing bowl and add your cream cheese.
  6. Then, mix in half a cup of shredded cheese along with a pinch of paprika, a pinch of garlic powder, and salt and pepper.
  7. Mix in the cheese and spices into the cream cheese, then use a spoon or your hands to put the cream cheese mixture into each of the peppers.
  8. Fill the peppers until the cuts made can just barely close shut.
  9. Once all the peppers are full of cream cheese you can grab your bacon.
  10. Wrap each individual pepper tightly with bacon and lay onto a baking sheet. If you want to grill the peppers then I recommend putting tooth picks through them to hold the bacon in place on the grill.
  11. Put the peppers in the oven at 375 degrees Fahrenheit for about 20 minutes.
  12. You can see the peppers are getting close to being done when the bacon changes color. Continue cooking to achieve the desired crispiness of the bacon.
  13. Remove from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving.
