Beautifully Toasted Bread

Learn to make wonderfully toasted bread that tastes delicious and can be used as a supplement to any meal of anytime.

Toast Ingredients

  • Toaster
  • Bread of choice; preferably sourdough or Hawaiian rolls
  • Butter; salted or unsalted

Toast Preparation

  1. Grab a toaster and plug it into a wall socket.
  2. Take the bread of choice and insert it into the slots of the toaster.
  3. Set the temperature and time of the toaster and toast bread to desired doneness.
  4. Remove bread from toaster and grab a butterknife to slather butter on the top of the hot bread.
  5. Set the toast with anything else and it is now ready to serve or eat it by itself.
